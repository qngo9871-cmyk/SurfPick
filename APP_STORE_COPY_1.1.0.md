## Description
You've got an hour. Where do you go?

Surf Pick checks every break within reach and tells you the one to drive to right now. No graphs. No browsing. Just the spot.

NEW — ASK SIRI, HANDS-FREE
Heading to the coast? Just say "Hey Siri, Surf Pick surf check" and hear your best nearby break without lifting a finger — or pull down on your Home Screen and find it in Spotlight. The answer comes back spoken, with a ranked snippet, before you've even opened the app.

HOW IT WORKS
• Surf Pick finds the surf breaks nearest you
• It checks live conditions — swell, wind and tide — at each one
• It ranks them and shows you the best bet, first

DECISIVE, NOT DATA
Other apps hand you charts and make you decide. Surf Pick decides. The #1 break is front and centre, with a simple green / amber / red rating you can read at a glance, in the sun, from the car.

GET DIRECTIONS, FAST
Tap any break for one-tap directions in Google Maps or Apple Maps. Pick your default and go.

FREE + PRO
Free: your top 3 nearest breaks, ranked, with directions and ratings.
Pro (one-time, no subscription): all 10 nearest breaks, the Home Screen widget (which becomes a CarPlay widget), custom drive radius and saved locations.

Conditions data from Open-Meteo. Surf Pick is a planning aid — always check the surf and your own ability before you paddle out.

Get in the water.
